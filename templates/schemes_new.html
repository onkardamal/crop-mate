A comprehensive scheme for the development of food processing sector in the country.

Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana

Crop insurance scheme to provide financial support to farmers in case of crop failure.

Benefits
  • Low premium rates (1.5% to 5% of sum insured)
  • Coverage for all crops and all risks
  • Quick claim settlement
  • Use of technology for assessment
Eligibility

All farmers growing notified crops in notified areas

Contact Information
